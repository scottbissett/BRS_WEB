What we provide.

Building Research Solutions provides an on-demand research and development department that offers bespoke science-oriented building solutions through our combined expertise.Our services can be employed at any stage of the construction life cycle to prov‘actionable intel’; capturing the data needed to make informed, actionable decisions.

We specialise in delivering decision support tools, capturing, analysing, synthesising and interpreting data to allow clients to achieve their objectives. Building Research Solutions is built around four themes - 'Energy ergonomics', ‘Building physics’, ‘Asset surveying’, and ‘Sustainable Communities’ and they complement each other to provide a holistic service for your project.

Scroll down to see more about our areas of expertise.

Energy Ergonomics.

Energy and carbon consultation.

Seamlessly working sustainability and energy efficiency into the design stage of your building’s life cycle. Computational modelling of your building(s) to simulate energy, carbon and cost parameters, delivering multiple scenarios to inform your decision making.

Building Sectors:

Architectural designers | Clients of design projects | House builders | Asset Managers | Real Estate Surveyors

Research Workflow:

At Design Stage | Concept Design | Earlier the Better

Solutions:

Alignment with Scottish Building Standards Section 7 | Building Performance Evaluation | Design stage low energy validation | Life Cycle Assessment | Life Cycle Inventory | Domestic and Non-Domestic | Desktop study | Digital twin

Building Physics.

Ensuring optimal performance.

Applying the fundamentals of physics to your project. Using scientific apparatus to provide you with insights into the workings of your building(s). Capturing, analysing and interpreting in-situ measured data, delivering intel on life cycle assessment and energy performance of new build and retrofit (post-war, historic/traditional, recent, deep and lite-touch) construction.

Building Sectors:

Facility Managers | Local Authorities | Registered Social Landlord | Private Consultants | SMEs and System Developers | House Buildings | Asset Managers

Research Workflow:

During & post construction| Pre and post occupation | Life cycle monitoring any age of building

Solutions:

Pre-occupation evaluation | Build Quality Spot Checking | Partners Energy Ergonomics Through Digital Twin | Provide Remedial Strategies | U-Value Testing | Air Tightness Testing | Energy Monitoring | quality control spot checks during construction phase.

Sustainable Communities.

Bringing people together.

Working with your stakeholders to validate your decision, through the lens of human factors . Providing you with actionable intel for the people that live in (or near) your buildings.

Building Sectors:

Community groups | Not for Profit Organisations | Housing Associations | Local Authorities

Research Workflow:

Goal and Scope | Strategic Planning | KPI setting | Project Preparation

Solutions:

Focus Groups | Questionnaires | Opinion Polls | Satisfaction Surveys | Building User Surveys (BUS) | Occupant Thermal Comfort

Asset surveying.

Realising value.

Applying our state-of-the-art surveying technology to capture information about your building(s) and/or area to be developed. Capturing and developing Photogrammetry and LIDAR scans into an as-built real-world digital model, to inform the creation of a digital twins, and provide accurate models for sizing.

Building Sectors:

House Builders | Civil Contractors | Heritage Sector | Conservation | Registered Social Landlords | Asset Managers

Research Workflow:

Concept Design | Spatial Coordination | Building Benchmarking | Pre-Intervention Measurements

Solutions:

InfraRed Thermography | Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (Drones) | PfCO certified | Building Information Modelling (BIM) | Digital Twin
