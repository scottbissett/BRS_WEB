Who we are.

Who

we

are.

Our team at Building Research Solutions is a collective of construction professionals; with a background in academia, we now provide scientific-rich solutions for the whole construction sector throughout every life cycle stage.

We offer discernible, pre-eminent knowledge in sustainability to advance the construction sector, working with the industry to reach its goals. Linked to the Scottish education sector, we bring cutting edge, scientific knowledge from the innovative labs of academia to industry and businesses.

What we Provide.

What

we

provide.

Building Research Solutions provides an on-demand research and development department that offers bespoke science-oriented building solutions through our combined expertise. Our services can be employed at any stage of the construction life cycle to prov‘actionable intel’; capturing the data needed to make informed, actionable decisions.

We specialise in delivering decision support tools, capturing, analysing, synthesising and interpreting data to allow clients to achieve their objectives. Building Research Solutions is built around four themes - 'Energy ergonomics', ‘Building physics’, ‘Asset surveying’, and ‘Sustainable Communities’ and they complement each other to provide a holistic service for your project.
